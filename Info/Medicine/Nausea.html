Nausea

    1. Ginger

     You can use it in any form, and ginger is great for morning sickness and motion sickness. ( Ginger )


    2. Peppermint

     If you have digestive nausea, drinking peppermint tea, eating peppermint candies, or smelling peppermint oil can help. ( Peppermint )


    3. Smiling

     I have not tried it but smiling is mentioned online to help with nausea.


    4. P6 pressure point

     3 finger widths below your palm on your wrist, apply pressure for 60 seconds.
