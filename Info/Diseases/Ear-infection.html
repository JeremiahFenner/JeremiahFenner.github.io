Ear Infection







Causes





   An ear infection is always caused by bacterial or fungal growth in the ear canal. This can happen from many sources most comonly water trapped in the ear allowing bacterial growth, or external contaminates in the ear.







Solutions





    1. Olive Oil

     Use organic extra virgin olive oil and add a few drops to the ear canal to soften ear wax and ease inflammation.




    2. Garlic Oil

     Garlic is anti-inflammitory and antimicrobial, add a few drops of warm garlic-infused oil to the ear canal. (simmer garlic in olive oil, then strain and cool)
