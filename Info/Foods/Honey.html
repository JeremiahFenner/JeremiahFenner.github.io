Honey

    1. Wound healing

     Antibacterial properties are good for wound healing (healing balm).


    2. Allergy relief

     Local honey can help sooth pollen allergies (Allergies).


    3. Immunity

     Honey is a great immune system booster, and honey with lemon can help sinuses (Congestion).


    4. Perservative

     Honey is a great perservative because it does not go bad, grow mold, or bacteria.
