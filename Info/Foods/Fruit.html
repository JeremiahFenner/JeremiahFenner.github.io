Benefits

    1. Immune system

     Fruits are very benefical to your immune system because they have a lot of vitamins, minnerals, and antioxidants. Fruit support cells and reduce inflammation and oxidative stress.


    2. Sun burn treatment

     I have a very light complexion and I got a very bad burn one day, At the time I had been eating very healthy for about 1 year and was experimenting with consuming a large ammount of rasberries and blackberries. I recovered from the burn completely in 3 days and I had no peeling at all, This was very uncommon for a severe sun burn for myself. After some deep thinking I realized the main change in my diet was from the intake of berries. The consumption of the antioxidants lowered my inflammation and boosted my immune system to where I was able to recover extreemly quickly with no permanent damage and for this reason I highly suggest this as a sunburn treatment.


    3. Antioxidants

     These help neutralize unstable molecules, which you can get from a variety of sources, UV, environment, smoking, processed foods.


    4. Fiber

     This is the key to fruit, fibers role slows digestion and limits blood sugar spikes. Natual fiber is also good for your gut microbiome.



Misconceptions

    Fruits contain a lot of sugar are they unhealthy?

Some fruits are unhealthy but it is not because of their sugar alone. Many fruits contain a significant amount of fiber and fiber will slow the digestion limiting blood sugar spikes. I consider fruits like watermelon that have low fiber, nutrients and high ammounts of sugar unhealthy. Watermelon also ranks very high on the glycemic index. However I believe most fruts are healthy you should always buy organic and pay attention to the ammount of fiber and nutrients they contain, fiber content being the most important.
