Avoid

    1.Processed foods:

     Processing food has a two tone effect, the first being that nutrients are destroyed during high heat and manipulation. For example a raw steak will have more nutrients than one well done. Also there are a lot of contaminants added to food during processing, In clean facilities this can be dust, and mold left on the machine or in the building, However in dirty facilities this can be as bad as machines that are never cleaned and pests. If you are making a switch to become healthier the first thing to cut out is processed foods. To note when you cut out processed foods you will be subsiquently avoiding many of the other things on this list as well.

Our bodys are great at adjusting to almost anything so you may not feel sick when you eat in-organic fruits and vegtables but if you quit them for two months and try to eat them again you will feel sick because your body has adjusted to healthier food. If you cannot afford organic fruits and vegtables washing them in baking soda can help remove a large ammount of contaminants. Also just becuase the food doesn't make you feel sick does not mean it is not making you sick.

Avoiding cane sugar can be very difficult in the modern world but the way you do it is by cutting out things from your diet or swapping them for alternatives one by one until all of it is gone. Date sugar is a great alternative to cane sugar.
