    4. Cleaning

     Lemon can be used to clean countertops and cutting boards because of its antibacterial properties and the citric acids ability to break down grease. On counter tops and cutting boards, cut a lemon in half and use half as a scrubber directly. Then wipe down with a paper towel. For sinks powder with baking soda and use half a lemon to scrub as well. This is very effective at disinfecting while leaving a pleasant smell.
