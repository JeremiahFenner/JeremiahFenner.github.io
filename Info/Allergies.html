Causes

   Allergies are an immune system response to an environmental toxin and they can be caused by many things including mold, water, pets, plants, dust and more. Most of the time these toxins are not harmful to your body but your immune system overreacts because they treat them like any other toxin.

Solutions

   If the toxin that is causing the allergy is harmful like mold, then you need to eliminate the toxin. However if it is not harmful like pollen then it is perfectly okay to calm your immune system to the response. The other way to heal allergic reactions is to expose your body to the source in a safe way.
